Ghana Tech and Business Hubs' Network

Welcome to the beating heart of technology, innovation and entrepreneurship in Ghana: "the gateway to Africa".

Who we are

The Ghana Hubs Network is the umbrella body of tech and innovation hubs, set up to support and promote collaboration of hubs to build a strong entrepreneurial ecosystem. The objectives of the initiative are:

  • Highlight actions by ecosystem partners which are addressing challenges faced by entrepreneurs.
  • Disseminate research findings to a wider audience and influence ongoing policy action.
  • Identify areas of collaboration and opportunities for ecosystem growth.
  • Share learnings and inform future programming to create value to GTBHN members.

Mission & Core Values

Our mission is to dvocate for the right policies for an enabling environment and help grow traction for our sustainable operations and lead the development of a cohesive and strengthened tech and business eco-system in Ghana.

These values underlie our work, how interact with each other, and which strategies we employ to fulfill our mission.

Integrity

We are ethical and transparent in our operations.

Inclusiveness

We respect people, value diversity and are committed to equality.

Quality

We strive for excellence through continuous improvement.

Collaboration

We are committed to a culture of teamwork and partnership.
